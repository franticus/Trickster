Privacy Policy — Trickster

At Trickster, we value your privacy as much as your skateboarding journey. Protecting your personal information is key to building trust with every rider in our community. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, the steps we take to protect it, and the options you have regarding your data. Our goal is to keep your experience safe, transparent, and inspiring while you explore tricks and challenges.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Trickster, we may collect some information to ensure a smooth and personalized experience. Certain details are provided directly by you — for example, when you register or contact us — while others are gathered automatically by the app to improve performance and usability. We also use cookies and local storage to keep your preferences and ensure continuity across sessions.

  • Your name, username, or email (only if you choose to register or contact us).
  • Basic device data such as browser type, operating system, and IP address.
  • Usage data like completed tutorials, tricks practiced, time spent, and interaction patterns.
  • Cookies or local storage to support functionality and save your settings.

2. Why We Collect This Data

The information we collect helps us keep the app reliable, engaging, and tailored to your needs. It allows us to identify which tutorials and features are most effective, improve the content, and fix technical issues faster. Data is also used to make sure Trickster remains compliant with applicable laws and standards.

  • To personalize your skateboarding experience and track progress.
  • To improve tutorials, challenges, and features based on user feedback.
  • To maintain platform performance and resolve issues quickly.
  • To comply with data protection and app-related regulations.

3. How We Protect Your Information

Securing your information is a top priority. We use trusted technologies and secure servers to prevent unauthorized access. Only authorized team members may access limited data when necessary for support or improvements. Our systems are regularly reviewed to meet strong security standards, and in the rare event of a security issue, we act promptly and transparently.

4. When We Share Information

Trickster never sells or misuses your data. Sharing happens only in limited situations essential for app functionality, such as analytics or hosting services. If required by law, we may also share information with relevant authorities. In all cases, only the minimum necessary data is disclosed.

  • Anonymous usage data with analytics providers to improve performance.
  • Hosting and cloud providers to ensure stability and reliability.
  • Legal authorities, only when required by law.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You remain in full control of your data. You may request access to the information we store, correct any inaccuracies, or ask us to delete your data if you no longer wish us to keep it. You can also limit or opt out of optional data use, such as analytics or communication updates. Just reach out to us, and we will handle your request quickly and transparently.

To contact us, email tricksterapp@gmail.com and our team will respond with care and clarity.

6. Communication and Updates

From time to time, we may send updates about Trickster — such as new tutorials, feature launches, or community events — but only if you have chosen to receive them. Every message will include an unsubscribe option, so you stay in full control of your inbox.

7. Compliance with Laws

Trickster follows major data protection frameworks, including the UK GDPR and similar privacy standards. We regularly update our practices to stay compliant with current regulations. This way, skaters can focus on learning and practicing, while knowing their personal information is handled responsibly.

8. Contacting Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests about this Privacy Policy, our team is here to help. Contact us anytime at tricksterapp@gmail.com. Whether it’s feedback, privacy inquiries, or general support, we value your input and are dedicated to maintaining your trust.
